## Key Features Shown:

1. **Large Item Buttons**: Easy-to-tap 120dp height cards with emoji icons
2. **Running Total**: Always visible subtotal and item count
3. **Grid Layout**: 3-column layout for maximum visibility
4. **Clear Cart & Checkout**: Prominent action buttons
5. **Clean Design**: Professional color scheme with high contrast

- **Visual Feedback**: Card elevation and selection states
- **Accessibility**: High contrast, large touch targets
- **Efficiency**: One-tap item addition, clear cart management
